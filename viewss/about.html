About US

This site is a website is a platform to spread awareness for community and individual sustainability on the university campus. It will serve as the introduction site for our Ashesi Goes Green webapp which will be used to calculate community sustainability and individual sustainability. Our perception-enabled technology is designed to address the sustainability needs of university campuses. Our technology utilizes advanced sensors to gather data and enable autonomous decision-making that promotes sustainability. By incorporating real-time information, we strive to create a safer and more sustainable campus environment.
